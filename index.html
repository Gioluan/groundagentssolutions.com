About

Travel experts turned tech builders

Ground Agents Solutions S.L. is a group travel and technology company based in Spain. Since 2005 we've been the on-the-ground experts for international sports teams visiting the country — organising training camps, friendly matches, and cultural tours from start to finish.


That hands-on experience led us to build the digital products we wished existed. Today we operate across group travel, sports club management, and consumer apps — every product born from a real problem we've lived firsthand.

"20 years on the ground organising tours taught us exactly what tools were missing — so we built them."
