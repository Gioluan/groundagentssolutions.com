02 · Clear space

Give it room to breathe

Keep clear space around the monogram equal to half its height (the gridded margin below). Never crowd the mark with text, edges, or other logos.

06 · Usage

Keep it consistent

Do

  • Use the full-colour lockup on dark or light backgrounds.
  • Preserve the clear space on all four sides.
  • Use the monogram for avatars, favicons and tight spaces.
  • Keep the gradient angle at 135° (top-left to bottom-right).

Don't

  • Recolour, rotate, or add effects to the monogram.
  • Stretch, condense, or change the wordmark font.
  • Place the lockup on a busy photo without a scrim.
  • Re-order the gradient stops or swap in other colours.
